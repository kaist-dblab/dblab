GENERAL INTRODUCTION

 


Database and Multimedia Lab (Adviser: Professor Kyu-Young Whang) has two main streams of reseach activities. First, we work on next-generation DBMSs including Object-Relational DBMSs, Advanced Data Storage Systems, XML DBMSs, DBMSs tightly coupled with Information Retrieval (IR), Spatial DBMSs and GISs, Distributed Multi DBMSs, Main-Memory DBMSs, Mobile DBMSs and Ubiquitous Computing, Embedded DBMSs, Web DBMSs, Stream DBMSs, and Engineering DBMSs. Besides, we work on advanced applications such as Parallel Search Engines (like Google), Data Warehousing, Data Mining, Digital Library, e-Commerce, and Information Retrieval. Second, we work on multimedia, especially on Multimedia Information Systems, Multimedia and Hypermedia Information Exchange (MHEG), and Multimedia Authoring Tools.
